Hi! I'm Kayla

I'm is an interdisciplinary artist who earned my BA from the Lamar Dodd School of Art at the University of Georgia. My artistic journey spans diverse mediums, including graphic design, photography, textile work, and sculpture, each contributing to a rich and evolving creative skill set. As an interdisciplinary artist, I embrace the freedom to explore and merge multiple creative paths. This approach allows me to challenge traditional boundaries and strengthen my ability to engage in projects with multiple creative perspectives.
