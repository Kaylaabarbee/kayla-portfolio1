Hey, I'm Kayla!

Kayla Barbee is an interdisciplinary artist who earned her BA from the Lamar Dodd School of Art at the University of Georgia. Her artistic journey spans diverse mediums, including graphic design, photography, textile work, and sculpture, each contributing to a rich and evolving creative skill set. As an interdisciplinary artist, Kayla embraces the freedom to explore and merge multiple creative paths. This approach allows her to challenge traditional boundaries and strengthen her ability to engage in projects with multiple creative perspectives.
